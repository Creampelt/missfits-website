Destination: Deep Space

Destination: Deep Space, the 2019 game for FRC, centers around a space theme in which robots on each alliance must collect and deposit "cargo" (orange rubber balls) and "hatch panels" (polycarbonate disks), as well as complete a climb onto the "Hab" (an elevated platform) in order to earn points. More information about the game can be found on Wikipedia.
